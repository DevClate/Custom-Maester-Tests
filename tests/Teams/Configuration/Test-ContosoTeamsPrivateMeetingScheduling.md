This test reviews whether private meeting scheduling is enabled.

Private meetings are not visible on users' calendars. This informational test reviews the status of private meeting scheduling to ensure it aligns with organizational meeting visibility requirements.

**To modify this setting:**

- Navigate to the [Teams admin center > Meeting policies](https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/policies/meetings)
- Select the Global (Org-wide default) policy
- Review the **Private meeting scheduling** setting
- Enable or disable based on your organization's requirements
- Click **Save**

- [Manage meeting policies in Teams](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoftteams/meeting-policies-overview)
