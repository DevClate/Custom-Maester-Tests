This test checks if stickers are disabled in the Teams messaging policy.

Contoso's company policy requires stickers to be disabled as they can contain inappropriate content and are unprofessional in business communications. This is important for maintaining a professional workplace environment.

**To remediate this issue:**

- Navigate to the [Teams admin center > Messaging policies](https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/policies/messaging)
- Select the Global (Org-wide default) policy
- Set **Use Stickers** to **Off**
- Click **Save**

**Learn more:**

- [Manage messaging policies in Teams](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoftteams/messaging-policies-in-teams)
- [Teams messaging policies reference](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoftteams/messaging-policies-in-teams#messaging-policy-settings)
