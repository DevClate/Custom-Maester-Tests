**To review and modify settings:**

- Navigate to the [Teams admin center](https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/)
- Review:
  - [Meeting policies](https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/policies/meetings) for anonymous access

- Adjust settings based on your security requirements
- Click **Save** on each page
