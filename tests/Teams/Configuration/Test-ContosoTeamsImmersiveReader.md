This test reviews the status of Immersive Reader in Teams.

Immersive Reader is an accessibility feature that helps users with reading and comprehension. This informational test reviews whether the feature is enabled for your organization.

**To modify this setting:**

- Navigate to the [Teams admin center > Messaging policies](https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/policies/messaging)
- Select the Global (Org-wide default) policy
- Review the **Immersive Reader** setting
- Enable or disable based on accessibility requirements
- Click **Save**

- [Manage messaging policies in Teams](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoftteams/messaging-policies-in-teams)
