This test checks if broadcast recording settings are appropriate for your organization.

Contoso's company policy requires broadcast recording settings to be reviewed based on compliance and data retention requirements. This is important for ensuring that live event recordings meet organizational standards while considering storage costs, privacy implications, and regulatory compliance.

**To review this configuration:**

- Navigate to [Teams Admin Center - Broadcast Policies](https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/policies/broadcast).
- Review the **BroadcastRecordingMode** setting in the Global policy.
- Consider your organization's requirements:
  - **Compliance**: Some regulations require recording of live events.
  - **Storage**: Recordings consume significant storage space.
  - **Privacy**: Recordings may contain sensitive information.
  - **Data Retention**: Recordings must comply with retention policies.
  - **Access Control**: Consider who should have access to recordings.
- Possible values:
  - **Always**: All broadcasts are automatically recorded.
  - **UserOverride**: Organizers can choose whether to record.
  - **AlwaysDisabled**: Recording is not allowed.
- Adjust the setting based on your organization's needs.
